Our Story

Where moonlight meets flour

Moonlit Café & Bakery was born from a simple dream: to create a space where people could escape the chaos of everyday life and enjoy the simple pleasures of freshly baked goods and expertly brewed coffee.

Every recipe is meticulously crafted, using only the finest ingredients. From our signature sourdough to our decadent cheesecakes, we pour our hearts into every creation.
